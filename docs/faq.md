# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Here are some common questions and answers about Team3_Rev1.

## How do I reset my password?

If you need to reset your password, follow these steps:
1. Go to the login page.
2. Click on "Forgot Password?"
3. Follow the instructions sent to your email.

## What is the recommended way to report a bug?

To report a bug, please use the GitHub issues tracker located at:
https://github.com/yourusername/team3_rev1/issues

Ensure you describe the problem thoroughly, including steps to reproduce, expected outcome, and actual outcome.
